Privacy Policy

This site collects the destination fax number submitted for a test, technical request data such as IP address for rate limiting, and delivery events returned by the fax provider.

Fax provider credentials are stored only on the server. Do not submit sensitive personal, medical, financial, or legal documents through this tool. The default test page contains no private customer data.

If advertising is enabled, advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance and show relevant ads. Privacy or data questions can be sent to contact@faxsignal.com.
